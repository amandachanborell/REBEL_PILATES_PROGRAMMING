Class Intro
Welcome to Forge. Today we're working with the Magic Circle and Dumbbells, building strength through resistance-based movements that challenge the full body in a way that's controlled and intentional. It's not about pushing to failure. It's about working at a level that makes you feel strong and powerful. Let's go.
