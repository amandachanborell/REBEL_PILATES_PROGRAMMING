Class Intro
Welcome to Forge. Today we're building strength with the Short Box, Pole, and Dumbbells — using springs and resistance-based props to challenge the full body in a way that's controlled and intentional. It's not about pushing to failure. It's about working at a level that makes you feel strong and powerful. Let's go.
