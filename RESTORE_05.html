Class Intro
Welcome everybody to Restore. Today we're working with the Box and Booty Band, moving through a full body session focused on hip stability, spinal mobility, and single leg control. Expect some long box sequences, rotational work through the spine, and plenty of glute and hip loading. Take your time, breathe through it, and as always, listen to your body.
