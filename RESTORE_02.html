Class Intro
Welcome everybody to Restore. Today we are working with the Pilates Ball and Box, and we will be moving slow and controlled through a full body session focused on mobility, stability, and hip flexibility. Expect some deep stretching, rotational work through the spine, and plenty of single leg loading. Take your time, breathe through it, and as always, listen to your body.
