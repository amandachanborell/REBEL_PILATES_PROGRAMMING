Class Intro
Welcome everybody to Restore. Today we're working with the Magic Circle and Loops, moving through a full body session focused on single leg stability, rotational control, and upper body mobility. Expect some hip and glute work, kneeling sequences, and a few standing challenges thrown in. Take your time, breathe through it, and as always, listen to your body.
