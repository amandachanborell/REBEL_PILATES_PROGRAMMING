Class Intro
Welcome everybody to Restore. Today we're working with the Box and Pilates Ball, moving through a full body session focused on single leg stability, thoracic mobility, and deep core control. Expect some ball-supported work, long box sequences, and a mix of flowing and grounding movements. Take your time, breathe through it, and as always, listen to your body.
